# Template: Explicit Handoff

**Use this** when work clearly belongs more in another role's primary ownership area.

---

**Write the following (or similar) clearly in the shared active-work file and/or in the session:**

---

**Handoff**

From: [Your Exact Role]  
To: [Target Exact Role, e.g. Receipt & Reconciliation Engineer]

**Why this handoff**:
[1-2 sentences explaining why the target role is the better owner]

**Current State**:
- Key file: `.grok/teams/live-16/active-work/<task>.md`
- Summary of what has been done so far: [brief]
- Current blocker or open question: [specific]

**What I am asking you to do**:
- [Take full ownership from here / Review and confirm approach / Implement the next piece / etc.]

**Please confirm receipt** with a short message ("Received — I am taking this forward") and continue the work using the Live Collaboration Protocol.

---

After writing the handoff note, the sending agent should also directly address the target role in the session so the Engineering Manager or Domain Lead can spawn them with proper context.
